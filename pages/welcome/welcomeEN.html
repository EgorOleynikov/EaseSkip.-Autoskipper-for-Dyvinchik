Thanks for installing the Ease Skip extension!

Tired of skipping the wrong age for you in Divinchik? Then this extension can help you!

  • Go to your dialogue with the VK bot Dyvinchik, turn on the extension;
  • Choose the minimum age in the questionnaires offered by the bot that suits you;
  • set a delay (Optional. This parameter determines the time after which the questionnaire will be analyzed. The larger the number, the less chances of meeting CAPCHA)

    • Now watch out how old age profiles pass by! When the age is right for you, the extension will wait for your decision and then continue. Enjoy. And you enjoy it quite a lot, consider supporting the author:.
